Research

IEEE VAST Challenge 2014 Mini-Challenge 1

Apr. 2014 – Jul. 2014

The challenge was on HERE. We developed a analysis tool consists of three views for this challenge: Resume Reader, News Timeline and Email Reader. Through interactions, analysts can discover the development of events and find the suspicious people in the events.

Dynamic K-Hop Reachability Queries

Jun. 2014 – present

Reachability queries is now an universal issue in large-scale graphs. Cheng et al. depicted k-hop reachability problem and gave an general solution to k-hop reachability queries. To efficiently handle the situation when the graph is dynamically modified, we tried to improve the index building process with the pruned landmark labeling algorithm.

On the Complexity of Binary Matrix Factorization

Apr. 2014 – Jun. 2014

Rank-one binary matrix factorization is often used in mining the patterns of binary data while preserving its discrete property. This project discussed the complexity of rank-pne binary matrix factorization problem and reformulated it as a variant of maximum edge weighted biclique problem or a biclique editing problem.
