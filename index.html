Biography

I am Zhengjie Miao, currently a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science, Duke University. I am working with Prof. Jun Yang and Prof. Sudeepa Roy on databases. Here is my curriculum vitae.

Before joining Duke, I received M.S. in Computer Science from Columbia University, where I’ve worked on interactive data analysis system under the advisory of Prof. Eugene Wu. I got my B.S. in Computer Science from Peking University, under the advisory of Prof. Xiaoru Yuan.
