Biography

I am Zhengjie Miao, currently a Master’s student in the Department of Computer Science, Columbia University. I am working with Prof. Eugene Wu in the Columbia University Database Research Group on enhancing data visualizations. My research interests covers the topics of information visualization, visual analytics, and data mining.

Before starting my graduate study at Columbia University, I worked as a research assistant in Visualization and Visual Analytics Group at Peking University, under the advisory of Prof. Xiaoru Yuan.
