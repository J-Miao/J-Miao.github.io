Biography

I am Zhengjie Miao (苗正杰), currently a Research Scientist at Megagon Labs. My research interest broadly lies in data management and artificial intelligence, with a focus on developing algorithms and tools for human-in-the-loop data science.

Previously, I finished my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Duke University, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Sudeepa Roy and Prof. Jun Yang. I received M.S. from Columbia University under the advisory of Prof. Eugene Wu and my B.S. from Peking University. I was a finalist of the Microsoft Research PhD Fellowship.
